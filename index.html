Console Shopping

Shopping 31173 style
- To add product item to the list simply type name of the product you want to buy and press Enter
- To delete product item to the list simply type delete and the number of the product and press Enter
- To mark a product items as purchased simply type done and the number of the product and press Enter
- To UNmark a product items as purchased simply type undo and the number of the product and press Enter
- To delete all product items from the list simply type reset and press Enter
